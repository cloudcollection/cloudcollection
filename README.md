## 🌍 Sustainable Tourism Capacity Assessment and Policy Optimization

_MCM/ICM 2025_

**Background**  
Overtourism has caused increasing pressure on environmental capacity and public infrastructure in tourism-oriented cities. This project aims to quantitatively evaluate tourism sustainability and support policy decision-making.↳

**My Role**

- Designed a **three-dimensional indicator system** (environmental, economic, and social dimensions)
    
- Conducted **indicator normalization and weight determination** using CRITIC and entropy methods
    
- Built a **composite sustainability index** based on TOPSIS and performed sensitivity analysis
    

**Methods & Tools**  
Multi-criteria statistical evaluation | CRITIC & Entropy weighting | TOPSIS | Sensitivity analysis  
Tools: MATLAB

**Key Findings**

- The composite index effectively reflects changes in tourism pressure
    
- Environmental governance indicators show the highest sensitivity
    
- The model structure demonstrates reasonable transferability (validated using a Macau case)

## 📈 Industrial Investment Structure and Sustainable Development Analysis in China

_HUASHU 2025_

**Background**  
This project investigates how industrial investment affects economic growth, employment, and environmental outcomes under long-term structural transformation.

**My Role**

- Conducted **time series stationarity tests (ADF)** on investment data from 1979–2023
    
- Built **multiple regression models** to estimate marginal effects of industry investment
    
- Participated in scenario analysis based on multi-objective optimization results
    

**Methods & Tools**  
Time series analysis | ADF test | Multiple linear regression | Multi-objective optimization  
Tools: Python / MATLAB

**Key Findings**

- Investment effects vary significantly across industries
    
- New energy and high-technology sectors exhibit better overall performance in most scenarios
    
- Results remain stable under moderate parameter perturbations
